about us

Personal recommendations: Nearly half of diners rely on suggestions from friends and family, making word-of-mouth one of the most trusted sources. Online reviews: Platforms like Google Reviews, Yelp, and Facebook are widely used, with 92% of people reading reviews before choosing a restaurant. Google Reviews is the most popular due to its integration with search results. Search engines: Around 40% of consumers use Google to find restaurants, especially with "near me" searches, which have grown significantly. Social media: Platforms like Instagram and Facebook help users discover restaurants through posts, stories, reels, and community recommendations. Algorithms also suggest dining options based on user behavior.
